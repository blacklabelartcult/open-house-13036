About This Home

The Real Story

Here's the deal: someone built their dream home — midcentury design, but with the kind of space modern life actually needs — and now you get to buy it.

4 beds. 3 full baths. 3,056 square feet on nearly a quarter acre in Paradise Valley Village. Built in 2024, so everything is new. Everything works. No deferred maintenance. No "good bones" cope.

The kitchen has a party pantry and an appliance galley — which means you can cook for 20 people without your counters looking like a war zone. The living spaces are big and they flow the way you want: open where it matters, defined where it counts.

Head up to the rooftop deck. Mountain views. City lights. That's your evening. Step outside at ground level and you've got 1,000 square feet of covered outdoor space with a fire feature — this is the Phoenix backyard you actually use year-round.

There's a detached casita for guests, in-laws, or your home office that needs a real door and its own walls. The garage is oversized, climate-controlled, and has extra height — if you care about your cars, this matters.

No HOA. Cul-de-sac. Desert preserve borders the lot. Mountain views front and back. Property taxes are $569 a year. Read that again.

This one checks the boxes. Come see it.
